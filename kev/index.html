Jared McCormack.

Writer,teacher,podcaster.

Jared Mccormack was born in small-town Missouri.After earning a bachelor's degree in English Education, he sold his car and used the money to move across the world over the next 8 years, he lived, he taught, and wrote in taiwan, argentina, spain, and south korea. He is currently persuing his MFA in creative writing from the university of Missouri-Kansas City.
